## Levels

Level | Procedure | Code
--- | --- | ---
Permanent | `logger.log_permanent` | 0.5%
Error | `logger.log_error` | 5%
Warning | `logger.log_warning` | 1%
Information | `logger.log_information` | 1%
Debug |  `logger.log` | 90%
Off | _N/A_ | _N/A_
